Privacy Policy - AskLLM

Local Storage

The API key you enter is stored locally on your device and is only used to authenticate directly with the LLM provider's API when you make requests. It is never shared with our servers or any third parties.

Device-Specific Storage

Even if you use the same browser across multiple devices, you must provide the API key separately on each device. This ensures that your API key remains secure and private.

Data Transmission

When you click the "Summarize" button or ask a question, the content is transmitted directly to the LLM provider's API to generate a response.

Privacy Assurance

If no action is taken, no data will be transmitted anywhere, ensuring your privacy is maintained.

Data Handling

AskLLM does not have access to any of your data.

Model-Specific Policies

Your use of LLM provider's services is governed by their respective terms of service and privacy policy.

Note: This policy ensures your data remains under your control at all times.
